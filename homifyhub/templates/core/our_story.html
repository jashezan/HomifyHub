Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

HomifyHub was founded with a simple idea: make home improvement accessible to everyone. We started with a small team and a big vision to transform how people shop for home improvement products.

2021

Rapid Growth

We expanded our product catalog to include thousands of items from trusted brands. Our customer base grew to over 10,000 satisfied homeowners across the country.

2022

Innovation Focus

We launched our expert consultation service and introduced AR visualization tools to help customers envision their projects before they begin.

2023

Sustainability Initiative

We committed to environmental responsibility by partnering with eco-friendly manufacturers and implementing sustainable packaging solutions.

2024

National Expansion

Today, we serve customers nationwide with same-day delivery in major cities and continue to innovate in the home improvement space.
