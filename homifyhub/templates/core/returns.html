Returns & Refunds

Return Policy Overview

30-Day Return Guarantee

We offer a 30-day return policy on most items. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories included.

Returnable Items

  • ✓ Unused tools and equipment
  • ✓ Home decor items
  • ✓ Hardware in original packaging
  • ✓ Appliances (unopened)
  • ✓ Building materials (unused)

Non-Returnable Items

  • ✗ Custom-cut materials
  • ✗ Electrical items (used)
  • ✗ Plumbing fixtures (installed)
  • ✗ Paint and chemicals
  • ✗ Personal protective equipment

Return Process

1

Contact Customer Service

Email us or call to initiate your return request.

2

Receive Return Label

We'll email you a prepaid return shipping label.

3

Package & Ship

Pack the item securely and attach the return label.

4

Receive Refund

Refund processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the item.

Refund Information

  • • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
  • • Shipping costs are non-refundable (except for defective items)
  • • Return shipping is free for defective or damaged items
  • • Exchanges available for size/color variations
  • • Store credit option available for faster processing

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us immediately. We'll provide a full refund or replacement at no cost to you.
